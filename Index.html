                      🧮 Decision Framework
                      when a job/career move is on the table, walk through these. honestly.
                      1. Does this move me toward the North Star above, or away from it?
                      2. Salary & trajectory — does it hit my money goal in 5 years?
                      3. Hours, location, flex — what do I actually have to give up?
                      4. Health impact — sleep, stress, body, mind. Honest answer.
                      5. Family time & relationships — what changes?
                      6. What will I learn? Who will I become in this role?
                      7. Worst-case scenario — could I live with it for 18 months?
                      8. If I do this for 3 years, will future-me be proud or just tired?
                      9. Is this a "Hell yes!" or just a "yes, I guess"?
                      10. Slept on it. What does my gut say in the morning?
